Step 1: Draft Your Question

The community is here to help you with specific coding, algorithm, or language problems. Opinion-based questions will be removed.

  • Include details about your goal.
  • Describe expected and actual results.
  • Any error messages.
Show what you’ve tried and tell us what you found (on this site or elsewhere) and why it didn’t meet your needs. You can get better answers when you provide research.
When appropriate, share the minimum amount of code others need to reproduce your problem (also called a minimum, reproducible example)
